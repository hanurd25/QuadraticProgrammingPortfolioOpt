# QuadraticProgrammingPortfolioOpt
Portfolio optimization with quadratic optimization in R

### Background:

We use the following model:

$$
min -w'*E(r) \lambda \cdot w \cdot \Omega \cdot w'
$$
$$
s.t. (subject to) lb \leq w \leq 0.05
$$

$$
\sigma w = 100 %
$$
